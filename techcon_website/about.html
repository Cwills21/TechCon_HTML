Our History

TechCon began as a local technology meetup in 2012, uniting developers, thinkers, and entrepreneurs passionate about shaping the digital future. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform where innovation, learning, and networking converge annually.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire innovation and promote collaboration across the global tech community. Our goal is to provide an inclusive and forward-thinking environment where ideas flourish and professionals grow.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovecode

Dr. Lovecode is a trailblazer in artificial intelligence ethics and transparency. At TechCon 2022, she presented her work on making AI systems accountable and explainable.

Elijah Byte

As the founder of ByteWorks, Elijah Byte advocates for sustainable and open-source technology development. His TechCon 2023 session emphasized the importance of community-driven innovation.

Rita Firewall

Rita is a leading expert in cybersecurity and has advised major global firms on threat prevention. Her TechCon appearances have focused on empowering everyday developers to build secure systems.
